Pune's vision is to be a vibrant, sustainable, and inclusive hub of economic growth and well-being, while preserving its cultural heritage. Its mission involves fostering collective action, channeling resources, and enabling collaboration between government, NGOs, corporations, and citizens to achieve this vision.
