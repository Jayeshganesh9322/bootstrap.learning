History:

Ancient and Medieval Periods: Pune has a history dating back to the Rashtrakuta and Yadava dynasties. Maratha Empire: Pune rose to prominence as the capital of the Maratha Empire under the Peshwas. British Raj: After the fall of the Peshwa rule in 1818, Pune became a major British military hub and later a part of the Bombay Presidency. Modern Era: The Pune Municipal Corporation was established in 1950, playing a key role in the city's development, especially after the Panshet flood of 1961. Smart City Mission: Pune was selected as a Smart City, focusing on improving infrastructure, technology, and citizen services. Intellectual Hub: Pune has a rich legacy in education, culture, and various industries, and the Pune International Centre (PIC) was established to foster intellectual engagement and policy dialogue.
